Note Detail

Word

serendipity

意外发现美好事物的能力；机缘巧合
Heard from a YouTuber explaining how he found his dream job.
Created: 06-09 · Not reviewed yet

AI Expansion

Chinese Definition

The occurrence of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way.

English Definition

Finding something good by accident while looking for something else.

Examples
  • It was pure serendipity that I found this book. 我在二手书店找到这本书纯属机缘巧合。
  • Scientific discoveries often involve serendipity. 科学发现往往包含着意外发现的成分。
  • Meeting her was the greatest serendipity of my life. 遇见她是我人生中最美好的意外。
Etymology

Coined by Horace Walpole in 1754, inspired by the Persian fairy tale "The Three Princes of Serendip".

Related Expressions

chance fortune happy accident
