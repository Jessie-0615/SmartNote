Word

serendipity

意外发现美好事物的能力；机缘巧合
Heard from a YouTuber explaining how he found his dream job — total serendipity.
Created: 06-09 · Not reviewed

AI Expansion

中文释义

指在寻找其他东西时，意外发现有趣或有价值事物的能力或运气。源于波斯童话。

English Definition

The occurrence of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way.

Examples
  • It was pure serendipity that I found this book. 我在二手书店找到这本书纯属机缘巧合。
  • Scientific discoveries often involve serendipity. 科学发现往往包含着意外发现的成分。
  • Meeting her was the greatest serendipity of my life. 遇见她是我人生中最美好的意外。
Etymology

源自1754年霍勒斯·沃波尔创造，受波斯童话《锡兰三王子》启发。

Related

chance · 偶然 fortune · 运气 happy accident · 美好意外
